## 1. Purpose

This protocol describes the complete lifecycle of a Community Domain from
creation to maturity.

Rather than describing individual engines, this protocol explains how every
engine interacts throughout the life of a Community Domain.

It becomes the master operational blueprint for GSN.

---

## 2. Constitutional Principle

A Community Domain is not a feature.

It is a living organisation operating on top of the GSN Trust Infrastructure.

Every engine exists to strengthen the Community Domain throughout its lifecycle.

---

## 3. Stage One - Community Establishment

```text
Community purchases Community Domain
-> Community Identity created
-> Community verified
-> Subscription activated
-> Community Settings configured
-> Governance Tree created
-> Community ready
```

---

## 4. Stage Two - Governance Deployment

```text
Executives appointed
-> Departments created
-> Sections created
-> Lines created
-> Permissions delegated
-> Administration activated
```

No members yet.

---

## 5. Stage Three - Membership Deployment

- Bulk Import.
- Invitation Codes.
- Phone Verification.
- Department Approval.
- Membership Activation.

Then:

```text
Community Directory completed
```

Trust has not started.

Only membership.

---

## 6. Stage Four - Community Activation

Members begin:

- Participating;
- Buying;
- Selling;
- Posting Spotlight;
- Creating Demand;
- Joining Committees.

Community becomes operational.

---

## 7. Stage Five - Behaviour Capture

Every meaningful activity becomes:

```text
Community Activity
-> Trust Event
-> Trust Passport
-> Trust Graph
-> Community Intelligence
```

Trust begins growing.

---

## 8. Stage Six - Opportunity Creation

Members publish:

- Spotlights;
- Demand;
- Shops;
- Vault;
- Merchant Releases.

Community begins producing opportunities.

---

## 9. Stage Seven - Opportunity Propagation

Opportunities travel.

```text
Community
-> Followers
-> Reposts
-> Other Community Domains
-> Cross-community Opportunity
```

Trust begins moving.

---

## 10. Stage Eight - Verification

Merchants verify.

Communities verify.

Employers verify.

Institutions verify.

Every verification response becomes:

- Trust Event.

Community Memory grows.

---

## 11. Stage Nine - Community Intelligence

Command Centre analyses:

- Participation;
- Trust Events;
- Marketplace;
- Finance;
- Leadership;
- Community Health;
- Opportunity;
- Trust Graph.

Community now understands itself.

---

## 12. Stage Ten - Institutional Memory

Leadership changes.

Departments change.

Members leave.

Members join.

Nothing disappears.

Trust Events remain.

Community Memory remains.

Trust Graph remains.

History remains.

---

## 13. Long-Term Cycle

```text
Community
-> Participation
-> Behaviour
-> Trust Events
-> Trust Passport
-> Opportunity
-> Prosperity
-> Stronger Community
-> More Participation
```

This becomes the permanent GSN Flywheel.

---

## 14. Engine Interaction

Community Engine creates structure.

Trust Engine creates evidence.

Marketplace Engine creates commerce.

Opportunity Engine creates visibility.

Community Finance Engine creates financial cooperation.

Knowledge Engine creates learning.

Infrastructure Engine supports everything.

No engine operates independently.

---

## 15. Constitutional Statement

The Community Domain Lifecycle represents the complete operating cycle of GSN.

Every Community Domain begins with governance.

Every member begins with membership.

Every Trust Passport begins with behaviour.

Every opportunity begins with trust.

Every prosperous community strengthens the Trust Infrastructure for the next
generation.

The lifecycle therefore represents the operational heartbeat of the GSN
Community Domain Operating System.
