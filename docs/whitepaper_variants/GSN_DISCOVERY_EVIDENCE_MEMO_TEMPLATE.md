## Stop / Pause Signal

```text
We should pause or redesign before pitching further if:
```

Examples:

- people cannot name a decision;
- people read GSN as social scoring;
- privacy concerns dominate value;
- the first buyer is still imaginary;
- the pilot depends on founder explanation;
- the community does not want to record evidence.

## Devil's Advocate

Do not turn isolated enthusiasm into a strategy. If only one person loves the idea, that is a lead, not validation. If five people name the same painful decision and the same acceptable evidence boundary, that is the beginning of proof.
