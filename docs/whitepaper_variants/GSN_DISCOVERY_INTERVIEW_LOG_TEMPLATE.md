# GSN Discovery Interview Log Template

Last updated: 2026-07-19

Use this during or immediately after each interview. The evidence memo is for synthesis later; this log is for raw capture before memory edits the facts.

## Start Reminder

Say:

```text
This is a discovery conversation, not a sales conversation. I am not testing you, and I am not asking you to agree with me. I need to understand what is real, what is risky, and what would make this useful or unacceptable.
```

## Do Not Clean This Up Too Soon

Raw notes are supposed to be messy. Keep the participant's own words. The job is not to make the interview sound intelligent; the job is to preserve what was actually said.
