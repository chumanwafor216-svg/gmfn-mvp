## Before We Start

This is a discovery conversation, not a test of you or the society.

You can disagree, skip a question, or ask not to be quoted.

Please do not share private details about another person in the room. If you
need to describe a situation, describe the pattern rather than naming someone.

The goal is to understand whether GSN could help a real community preserve
useful trust evidence without exposing private information or replacing human
judgement.
